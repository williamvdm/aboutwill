Culture of teamwork

Committed to code

Software is not about technology, it’s about the people who create it. People are unique and individual, and we understand that by mixing different talents within a team and giving them the space to be creative we produce the right environment to deliver great software. At Competa, everyone is welcome to contribute their ideas and influence is defined by initiative not job title. We’re passionate about coding, and love to learn, teach, share, build and have fun while we are at it.
